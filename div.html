Saleena

Born into a life of hardship in the bustling port town of Tortuga, Saleena Blackthorne learned to fend for herself from an early age. Orphaned by the age of ten, she survived by her wits and fierce determination, eventually finding a place among a crew of outlaws. Her quick mind and unmatched skill with a cutlass soon set her apart, earning her the respect and loyalty of her crewmates.

Finn

Finn Gallagher was born on a stormy night, the son of a notorious pirate captain and a tavern wench. His father was killed in a naval battle when Finn was only five, leaving him to fend for himself in the rough port town of Nassau. By the age of twelve, Finn had already made a name for himself as a cunning pickpocket and street fighter. He joined a pirate crew as a cabin boy and quickly rose through the ranks, eventually seizing command of his own ship, the Sea Serpent
